Uppidi Upload

A simple, cross-platform file uploader that works on your phone, tablet, desktop, or web browser. Upload images, videos, and other files to your choice of free public hosting services.

Download Now ⬇

Features

  • 9 free hosting providers — Catbox, FileDitch, Frisk, Litterbox, Uguu.se, TmpFile.link, FreeImage.host, TempSH, and more
  • Streaming uploads — handles large files without consuming excessive memory
  • Image quality selector — choose Original, Medium (50%), or Low (25%) compression
  • Image cropping — crop images before uploading
  • Upload history — view, copy, and share past uploads
  • Templated share messages — share links with customizable message templates
  • Provider health checks — test connectivity and enable/disable providers
  • Drag & drop — drag files onto the upload area on desktop
  • Cancel in progress — stop an active upload at any time
  • Dark & light theme — system-following theme with customizable seed colors
  • Multi-language — English, Turkish, and Italian
  • Cross-platform — Android, Linux, Windows, and web
