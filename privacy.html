Privacy

Privacy Statement

Uppidi Upload is designed with your privacy in mind. The application does not store, view, or keep copies of files you upload. Your files go directly from your device to the hosting service you select.

Data Handling

  • Uploaded files: Files are transmitted directly from your device to the chosen hosting provider. Uppidi does not intermediate, cache, or retain uploaded content.
  • Upload history: A local record of file names, hosting providers, and upload URLs is stored on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share a link.
  • Settings: App preferences (theme, language, proxy settings) are stored locally on your device.
  • No telemetry: Uppidi does not collect usage statistics, analytics, or crash reports.
  • No accounts: Uppidi does not require or support user accounts. There is no registration, login, or personal data collection.

Third-Party Services

Uppidi provides access to third-party file hosting services. Each service operates under its own privacy policy and terms of service. Please review the privacy policy of the hosting service you choose to use.

Updates

This privacy statement may be updated occasionally. The latest version is always available at this page.
